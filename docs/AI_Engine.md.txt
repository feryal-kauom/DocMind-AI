AI Engine
Overview

The AI Engine is the core intelligence layer of DocMind AI.

Instead of using a single prompt, DocMind AI follows a modular AI orchestration pipeline where every component has one clear responsibility.

This architecture improves accuracy, maintainability, scalability, and future extensibility.

AI Design Principles

Single Responsibility

Every module performs exactly one responsibility.

Modular Architecture

Every module can be replaced independently.

Explainability

The AI should explain its reasoning whenever possible.

Reliability

Responses should always be validated before reaching the user.

Scalability

New modules can be added without redesigning the pipeline.
